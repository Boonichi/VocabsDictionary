0. A small animal that is covered in fur and has a long thin tail. mice live in fields, in people’s houses or where food is stored. :
    . a field mouse a house mouse The stores were overrun with rats and mice.
    . She crept upstairs, quiet as a mouse .
    . ( figurative ) He was a weak little mouse of a man.
1. A small device that is moved by hand across a surface to control the movement of the cursor on a computer screen :
    . Click the left mouse button twice to highlight the program.
    . Use the mouse to drag the icon to a new position.
